Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Budgetable focuses on transparency and user control over personal finance data.

1. Data Collection

  • Budget, account, category, and transaction records you create in the app.
  • Preferences such as currency, language, and enabled features.
  • Optional AI provider configuration you decide to store.

2. Data Storage

  • Core finance data is stored on your device as local app data.
  • You control backup and restore through your device or platform settings.
  • Data remains local unless you explicitly trigger an external integration.

3. Data Sharing

Budgetable does not sell personal data. Data is shared only when required by features you choose to use, such as optional third-party AI integrations.

4. AI Features

If AI is enabled, selected content may be transmitted to your chosen provider for response generation. Provider handling is governed by that provider's own terms and privacy policy.

5. User Rights

  • You may modify or delete records in the app at any time.
  • You may disable AI providers and remove saved provider settings.
  • You may uninstall the app to remove local data from your device.
Local-first commitment: your financial records are primarily stored on your own device.
