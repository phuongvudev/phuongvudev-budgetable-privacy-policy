Privacy Policy

Your data privacy matters. Budgetable is designed with a local-first approach, and this policy explains what data is processed and how it is handled.

Local-first: your finance records are primarily stored on your device.

1. Data Collection

  • Budget, account, category, and transaction information that you enter.
  • App preferences such as currency, locale, and selected features.
  • Optional AI provider settings and API key configuration you choose to save.

2. Data Storage

  • Financial records are stored locally on your device database.
  • You control your device backups and data lifecycle.
  • Data remains on-device unless you explicitly use a feature that sends it externally.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data is only shared with third-party services when you explicitly enable and use those integrations (for example AI features).

4. AI Features

If you enable AI features, selected content may be sent to your chosen provider to generate responses. Provider behavior is governed by that provider's own policy.

5. User Rights

  • You can edit or delete your financial records in the app at any time.
  • You can disable AI providers and remove saved provider settings.
  • You can uninstall the app to remove local app data from your device.
Contact: privacy@budgetable.app
